Our Story

Gramixel Forge was founded by Samir Ziad Jweiles Baldizon while still in the 9th and 10th grades. His passion for technology started at an early age when he dreamed of creating video games and innovative websites. But, as a 9-year-old, no one took his ideas seriously.

Determined to bring his visions to life and to give others the opportunity to do the same, he built Gramixel Forge from the ground up. Now, Gramixel Forge helps individuals and companies develop their ideas and make them a reality, using the latest technologies in web and software development.
